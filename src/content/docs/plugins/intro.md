# Plugins

RCI Plugins are modular components designed to extend the functionality of our WordPress sites. They follow strict coding standards and are designed to be lightweight and performant.

## Available Plugins

### News Slider
A responsive, touch-friendly slider for displaying featured news items.
[Read Documentation](/docs/plugins/news-slider)

### Preheader
A customizable preheader bar for global announcements and navigation.
[Read Documentation](/docs/plugins/preheader)

## Development Standards
All plugins must adhere to the [Git Standards](/docs/workflow/git-standards) and should be developed with localization in mind.
